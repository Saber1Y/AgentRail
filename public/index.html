Problem

Agents stall at access walls.

APIs, browsers, and long-running tools are still locked behind subscriptions or manual approvals. That breaks the flow of agentic work.

Solution

Charge per task, not per month.

AgentRail makes the payment itself part of the workflow. Users pay only when the agent actually needs a tool or session.

Win condition

Demo the settlement, not just the prompt.

A sharp demo can show a real business outcome, a visible payment trail, and a memorable product story in under two minutes.

Rails

Two payment modes, one agentic experience.

x402 is the quick unlock for HTTP-sized actions. MPP is the longer session path for browser-driven or multi-step work. AgentRail wraps both in a single product narrative. 

prompt -> paywall -> tool -> deliverable
  x402     payment    agent      Stellar receipt
  MPP      session    browser     settlement
